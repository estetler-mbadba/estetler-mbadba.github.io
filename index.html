The fast track wins. The burner risks the major's 2.500-GPA gate by overloading quant courses, and leaves no room for the SIE exam, Bloomberg cert, or a junior-year internship — which are the actual differentiators on a finance resume.

Four simple rules

  1. Never pair two red (heavy) courses in the same 5-week block. That includes any two FINC-prefix or BSST-3083 + ECON-3003/3013.
  2. Every red gets a green partner — keep the cognitive load asymmetric.
  3. FINC-3083 (Investments) and FINC-3103 (Markets) stagger, never simultaneous. Their content overlaps, so doubling them feels efficient but actually reduces retention.
  4. Leave one 5-week block free in late 2027 to sit for the SIE exam. Don't let the schedule eat that window.
